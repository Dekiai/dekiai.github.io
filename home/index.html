LOG SOUND DESIGN

Hello there

My name is Logan Majstorovic. I studied Sound Design for video games and linear media at ISART Digital school in Paris from 2019 to 2022. There I had the opportunity to work on  a lot of projects that strengthened my teamwork experience, and confirmed my ambition to continue this path of career.

Sound, music and games have shaped my world since I was a little kid.

My skills cover all the tasks of a Sound Designer, from recording sounds to integrating them into game engines. I use Reaper, Wwise, Fmod, Unity, Unreal Engine and Pro Tools.

I specialize in Sound Design and Audio Implementation, especially for vehicle engines.
